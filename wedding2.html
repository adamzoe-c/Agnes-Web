The Wedding

Bespoke Three-Piece Tuxedo for Women – A Wedding Suit by Agnes Nordenholz

Laura commissioned a bespoke three-piece tuxedo for women, designed as a timeless alternative to a traditional wedding dress. The ensemble consists of a double-breasted tuxedo jacket, a tailored waistcoat, and straight-leg trousers, all cut to precise measurements and crafted using classic tailoring techniques.

For the civil wedding, we chose to reinterpret one of Laura’s existing tailored suits by adding a custom waistcoat in grey herringbone wool. The look was completed with a pleated tuxedo shirt, featuring traditional front pleats—an understated nod to formal eveningwear and sartorial heritage.

The main wedding celebration with friends and family took place in Greece, inspiring a lighter and more luminous material choice. For this occasion, the tuxedo was crafted from a fine plain wool selected from the Classic British Formalwear collection by Dugdale Bros. The fabric is a refined 280-gram wool, chosen in a cream tone, offering elegance, breathability, and structure—ideal for a warm Mediterranean setting.

Refined Details

To complete the design, we incorporated classic tuxedo details that define formal tailoring at its highest level:

  • A silk lapel on the jacket
  • Silk galon and a side stripe on the trousers
  • Traditional side adjusters instead of a belt

These elements reference historical menswear tailoring while being reinterpreted for a modern, feminine silhouette. The result is a wedding tuxedo for women that balances precision, restraint, and individuality—crafted to endure beyond a single occasion.

Each bespoke suit by Agnes Nordenholz is designed in Berlin and produced in close collaboration with specialized ateliers, emphasizing slow luxury, natural materials, and lasting craftsmanship. This tuxedo is not only a wedding garment, but a long-term wardrobe piece—intended to be worn, adapted, and lived in.

“I had the pleasure of having Agnes create my wedding suit and an additional waistcoat for my special days. The garments were impeccably tailored, and the entire experience was exceptional from start to finish. Agnes guided me through every step of the process... Her beautiful studio in Berlin reflects the pride and care she brings to her craft.”
— Laura

Thank you, Laura! Wishing you and Molly all the best.
