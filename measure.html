Made to Measure Tailoring for Women

Designed in Berlin, handmade by skilled artisans in Italy. We think that the future of fashion is something we already did in the past: making high quality tailored clothing for you. Let us introduce you to the world of classical tailoring, long known only as a service for men.

A made to measure suit for women is created to last – in quality, fit and relevance beyond seasons.

Designed in Berlin, Handmade in Italy

It is time for a change. A tailored suit for women is empowering, it gives you ease and confidence and allows you to express your personal style. The future of fashion is about style, not fashion.

We help you build a permanent and elegant wardrobe and experience the excitement of tailoring. An MTM suit for women is an investment. It is time to invest in yourself.

The Process

Consultation

We have our ladies’ pantsuits and tailored suits available for fitting in our custom tailoring shop in Berlin to help you decide which style suits you best.

Fabric Selection

Once you have decided on a model, we select the fabric together from our extensive collection of sample books from the finest Italian, French and British mills.

Measurements & Details

We then take all necessary measurements and define individual details such as lapel width, pocket design and length, creating a garment tailored precisely to your specifications.

Fitting & Completion

The order is sent to our manufacturing partner in Italy. Approximately 6–8 weeks later, a second fitting takes place, during which final adjustments can be made.
