Handcrafted Jewellery Inspired by Art

The inaugural collection of the gender-neutral jewelry line draws inspiration from Clement Kicklinger’s (1561-1617) art piece “The Ostrich Egg” housed at the Kunsthistorisches Museum in Vienna. Crafted entirely by hand in Pforzheim, known as the German jewelry capital, both the necklace and bracelet embody a fusion of rawness and contemporary flair in their gender-inclusive design.
