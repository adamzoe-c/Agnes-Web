Trunkshow Vienna

We cordially invite you to our upcoming

MADE TO MEASURE TRUNK SHOW in Vienna

26th February – 1st March 2026

The MADE TO MEASURE TRUNK SHOW in Vienna will take place twice a year at the atelier of Hedi Rochowanski in the 8th district of Vienna. We will offer private consultations by appointment, allowing you the tranquility to explore our collection at your own pace.⁠ ⁠

Our Made to Measure collection for women includes elegant coats, pant suits, jackets, vests, shirts, and tuxedos/smokings for women. We can’t wait to help you create a look that’s uniquely yours!⁠

Please subscribe to the newsletter to receive updates about our next dates.
